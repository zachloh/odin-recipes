Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A true Italian classic with a rich, meaty sauce. Perfect for a quick midweek meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g beef mince
  • 800g finely chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon oregano leaves
  • large pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 500g thin spaghetti
  • fresh basil leaves, to serve
  • parmesan cheese, shaved, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook onion, carrot, carrot, celery and garlic for 5 minutes.
  2. Add beef mince and cook for 6 to 8 minutes or until browned.
  3. Add pasta, tomatoes, oregano, nutmeg, sugar and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Meanwhile, cook pasta in boiling salted water, per package directions.
  5. Spoon sauce over pasta and serve topped with parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
