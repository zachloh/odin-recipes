Shepherd's Pie

Description

You can't go past a classic shepherd's pie for a satisfying Sunday lunch.That saucy, deep flavourful filling, that creamy potato topping, and that awesome golden cheese crust!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 500g lamb mince
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon plain flour
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 40g butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • melted butter, to brush

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrot and celery, and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add lamb mince and cook for 5 minutes or until it changes colour.
  3. Add the flour and cook, stirring for 2 minutes. Add beef stock, bay leaf, worcestershire sauce and tomato paste. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes or until sauce thickens. Taste and season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Cook potato in salted boiling water for 15 minutes or until tender. Add butter and mash until smooth. Add milk, salt and pepper and stir until combined.
  5. Preheat oven to 200࠷. Spoon lamb mixture into an ovenproof baking dish. Top with mashed potato and use a fork to spread over lamb mixture. Brush with melted butter.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until mashed potato is golden brown. Serve immediately.
